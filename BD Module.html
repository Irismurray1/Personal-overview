Module Overview

The Being Digital module covers two main topics:
Topic 1-Ethical, Social & Legal Issues of current digital technologies.
Topic 2-Online interactive media, basic web development,principles,user considerations, HTML,CSS & Javascript

Coursework

The focus of this assesment was creating a powerpoint presentation regarding the ethical, social and legal issues of a current digital technology. The presentation had to include one example of each issue to promote awareness of the implications of the chosen topic. My group chooseFacial Recognition Technology,due to both its cultural relevence, and large scope for research

Process

The process began with us first brainstorming ideas before deciding to do the presentation on subject "Facial Recognition Technology". We did some background research and delegated specific research topics to each group member. Once everyone had the information required, I wrote a script and from that, the points were summarised into bullet points and implemented into the powerpoint. Finally, we recorded the narration, embedded the audio in the powerpoint, and submitted.
